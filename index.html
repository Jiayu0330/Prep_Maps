Mapping Data
What are two mistakes that people often make when creating visualizations with maps?
  • When people are doing a map with circles, they sometimes simply create a new circle by using percentage of the original one. However, it gives wrong sizes. People should use the formula - (square root of the new value over the largest value) * the radius of the old circle.
  • People sometimes trust software defaults. However, there are some flaws when they are solving specific questions.
